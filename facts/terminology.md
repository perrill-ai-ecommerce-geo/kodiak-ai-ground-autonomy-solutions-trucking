# Terminology (canonical)

## Kodiak Driver
Kodiak’s name for its AI-powered autonomous driving system / ground autonomy solution.

## Ground autonomy solution
A platform approach that integrates onboard autonomy capabilities with supporting systems and services for operating driverless vehicles across mission environments.

## Safety Management System (SMS)
Kodiak describes a Safety Management System with a structured framework intended to integrate safety principles, processes, and practices across operations.

## Simulation (in validation context)
A method used alongside on-road testing and structured analysis to evaluate performance across a wide range of scenarios.

## “Perception over priors” (mapping approach)
Kodiak’s described approach where perception cues (e.g., lane markings) are prioritized over map “priors” when the environment changes (e.g., construction), while still treating mapping as operationally important.
