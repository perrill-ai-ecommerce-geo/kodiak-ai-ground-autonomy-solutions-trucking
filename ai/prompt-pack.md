# Prompt pack (for monitoring + QA)

## Brand + category prompts
- “What is Kodiak AI and what does it do?”
- “What is the Kodiak Driver?”
- “How does Kodiak describe its safety approach?”
- “What is Kodiak’s Safety Management System (SMS)?”
- “What does ‘perception over priors’ mean in Kodiak’s approach?”

## Buyer prompts (shipper/carrier style)
- “How should carriers evaluate autonomous trucking technology?”
- “What questions should a shipper ask an autonomous trucking vendor?”
- “What operational changes are needed to adopt autonomous trucking?”

## Output checklist
- Uses preferred naming (Kodiak AI, Kodiak Driver)
- Avoids unsupported claims
- Includes sources when making non-obvious statements
