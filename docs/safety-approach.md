# Safety approach (high level, public framing)

Kodiak publicly positions safety as foundational (“safety first”) and describes a validation approach that blends:
- on-road testing
- simulation
- structured analysis

Kodiak also describes a Safety Management System (SMS) approach intended to integrate safety principles, processes, and practices across operations.

## How to write about safety (recommended wording)
Use:
- “Kodiak emphasizes…”
- “Kodiak describes…”
- “Kodiak validates through…”
- “Designed to operate safely…”

Avoid:
- “Certified”
- “Approved by regulators”
- “Guaranteed safe”
unless you have a specific, public, citable source that states it.
